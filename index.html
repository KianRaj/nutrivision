Research preview · NutriVision

Single-image food nutrition estimation, with ingredient-aware frequency fusion.

Abstract

From one RGB plate photo we predict calories, mass, fat, carbs, protein — no depth sensor, no rig. A dual ConvNeXt-Base + IC-FAFM frequency fusion + IA-MPH head, conditioned on CLIP-encoded ingredients.

13.52 % Nutrition5k (new SOTA) · 10.89 % OmniFood8K (closes the gap with DA3-estimated depth).

Why this is hard

From one flat photo, both unknowns must come back:

  • What is on the plate  (ingredients).
  • How much of it  (3-D volume × density).

Geometry returns via estimated depth; semantics via ingredient text — fused in the frequency domain.

Contributions

  1. IC-FAFM — depth → low band · RGB → high band · CLIP-gated mix.
  2. IA-MPH — ingredient-gated head, 5 nutrients jointly.
  3. Two-dataset SOTA  13.52 % N5k · 10.89 % Omni.
  4. Single-image inference — RGB + ingredient text; depth estimated via DA3 (precomputed).
Method

Architecture

RGB and DA3 depth pass through a dual ConvNeXt-Base + ISM stack; CLIP-encoded ingredient text conditions both the frequency-domain fusion (IC-FAFM) and the prediction head (IA-MPH).

1

SSRA

Refines DA3 monocular depth via global scale-shift + a local CNN residual.

2

Dual ConvNeXt-B + ISM

Two ImageNet-22k pretrained backbones with window-attention refinement at 4 scales.

3

IC-FAFM

FFT split + ingredient cross-attention fuses RGB and depth at every scale.

4

IA-MPH

Ingredient-gated channel selection + cross-attention head predicts 5 nutrients.

Pipeline How one RGB photo becomes five nutrient numbers

Step 1

Input RGB

One 384 × 384 photo. No depth sensor, no rig.

3 × 384 × 384
Step 2

+ Depth (DA3)

DepthAnything V3 — relative depth from RGB. Warm = near, cold = far.

1 × 384 × 384 · raw float
Step 3

SSRA rescaling

Learns a per-image affine + small CNN residual to give depth a metric feel.

depth → scale-aware
Step 4

RGB features

ConvNeXt-B + ISM lights up on texture, gloss, char, leaf veins — calorie cues.

Frgb · 4 scales
Step 5

Depth features

Parallel ConvNeXt-B lights up on mound height, boundaries, occlusion — gram cues.

Fdep · 4 scales
Step 6

IC-FAFM fusion

2-D FFT splits each map. Depth → low band, RGB → high band; CLIP(ingredients) gates the mix.

Ffused · ingredient-aware
Step 7
